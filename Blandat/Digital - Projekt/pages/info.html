A bit about NaFiVa

What is NaFiVa?

A school project for Mid Sweden University that aims to create an online store for a fictitious / real company. This project became an online store for a company that sells its own products such as coffee and tea as examples.

History of the name NaFiVa

From the beginning this company was suppose to sell fishing rolls and hiking backpags. Na stands for nature, Fi fisking and Va for "vandring" which means hiking in swedish. Well... after a little google serach other ideas blow up and the plan had change. But the name NaFiVa was quite fitting for a coffe/tea brend, so we kept it and even after 14 years (read days) we still love the name.
